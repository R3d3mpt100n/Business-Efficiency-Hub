NOT replicate the official PDF exactly

“Educational Example – How to Fill Each Section”

A structured list of their information
Clearly labeled as:
“Information to transfer into official IRS Form W-7”

2. Safety & Compliance Constraints
Do NOT generate a filled official IRS form

Keep everything informational and instructional
